the education problem

Education is a large and nuanced field. Yet the quote above, taken from It’s Time to Build by Marc Andreessen, contains the essence of the challenge: quality and scale.

quality

More knowledge than Newton or Galileo could have dreamed of is accesible via the internet-connected devices in our pockets. We support projects that help unlock this potential.

scale

“We have top-end universities with the capacity to teach only a microscopic percentage of the 120 million new 18-year-olds in the world each year. Why not educate every 18-year-old? Isn’t that the most important thing we can possibly do?” Andreessen again. We couldn’t put it better. We support projects that aim to massively scale access to high-quality learning.
